Name

email.delimiters.enabled — Generate delimiters around email addresses?

Synopsis

<xsl:param name="email.delimiters.enabled">1</xsl:param>

Description

If non-zero, delimiters[6] are generated around e-mail addresses (the output of the email element).


[6] For delimiters, the stylesheets are currently hard-coded to output angle brackets.
